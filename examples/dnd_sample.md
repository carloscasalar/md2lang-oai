# The Goblin Cave

## Character Stats

- STR: 16 (+3)
- DEX: 14 (+2)
- CON: 12 (+1)
- INT: 10 (+0)
- WIS: 13 (+1)
- CHA: 8 (-1)

## Adventure Description

You enter a dark cave. The smell of rotting flesh fills your nostrils. Make a WIS saving throw or become poisoned.

The goblin attacks! Roll for initiative using your DEX modifier.
